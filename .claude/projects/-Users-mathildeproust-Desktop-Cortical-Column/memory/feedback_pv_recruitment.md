PV cells are recruited more strongly than E cells by feedforward thalamic (LGN) input in L4C. This is biologically established.

**Why:** User corrected a suggestion to flip E/PV drive balance. PV cells have faster, more reliable thalamocortical responses.

**How to apply:** When designing stimulus protocols, always maintain PV drive >= E drive from LGN. Gamma should emerge from the E-PV loop being activated by strong PV recruitment, not from selectively driving E over PV.
